INNERSOFT 2023


1st International Workshop on
InnerSource Software Development
Melbourne, Australia, In conjunction with ICSE 2023


Call for Papers

Our goal for the InnerSoft Workshop is to be the space for a productive dialogue between academics and practitioners interested in InnerSource. We are looking to minimize the known barriers to industry participation in software engineering research conferences. So, we are soliciting **extended abstracts, maximum 2 page long including references**, outlining a lightning talk or a poster presentation. All submitted abstracts will be peer-reviewed by members of the Program Committee and will be a part of the workshop proceedings.

We are soliciting abstracts in two categories:

  • • Problems: the talk outlines a specific problem in InnerSource adoption, which could be in an industrial context or is potentially interesting to an academic audience.
  • • Solutions: the talk reports on tools, techniques, or practices that could facilitate InnerSource adoption at an organization that the authors have built or are working on.
